About Us

We are young, dynamic and committed civil/electrical Engineering company. We strive to provide reliable, efficient and cost effective projects to many local and multinational companies on turnkey basis.


We adhere to the Engineering code of practice with HSE at the top and client's ultimate satisfaction. Our commitment with client is also backed up by extensive technical support, after sales service and comprehensive level of stock, for immediate response to our customer's requirements.


We work closely with our clients to enable more informed decisions and a streamlined delivery process. Having worked on numerous projects, with varying levels of complexity, we understand the challenges that must be overcome for a successful activation. From design-build and construction management projects, we take immense pride in the quality of service we provide and continue to be a leading force in industrial construction in Nigeria.
